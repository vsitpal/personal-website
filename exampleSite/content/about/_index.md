### Introduction

I’m a systems developer who is deeply interested in the principles that make complex systems tick. My work and curiosity naturally pull me toward operating systems, storage and database systems, computer architecture, networking, distributed systems, and the systems side of ML infrastructure. Over the years, I’ve also built experience with embedded systems, which only strengthened my appreciation for thoughtful engineering close to the metal.

I hold a master’s degree from Carnegie Mellon University, where I focused on Computer Systems & Security while also exploring machine learning and business management. Today, I work on Oracle’s core database kernel as part of the Virtual Operating Systems team in Redwood Shores, where I continue to sharpen my understanding of internals, performance, reliability, and elegant systems design.

In the long run, I hope to contribute meaningfully to open-source systems and infrastructure projects and stay actively involved in the broader open-source ecosystem—where strong ideas, careful engineering, and collaboration move technology forward.

`I love systems development, aircraft, trails, and bimmers.`
